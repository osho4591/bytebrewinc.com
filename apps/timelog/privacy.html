Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

Short version: TimeLog does not collect, transmit, or share any of your data. Everything stays on your device.

Overview

TimeLog ("the App") is a time tracking application developed by ByteBrew Inc. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the App handles your information.

Information We Collect

We collect no personal information. The App does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any identifying details.

The only data the App handles is the time-log entries you create yourself (dates, start times, and end times). This data is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to us or anyone else.

How Your Data Is Stored

All entries are saved to your device's local storage using Android's standard on-device storage APIs. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to ByteBrew Inc or any third party
  • Is removed when you uninstall the App or clear its data in your device settings

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your data with any third parties — because we never have access to it in the first place.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or tracking SDKs. No data is sent to external servers of any kind.

Permissions

The App does not request any sensitive device permissions. It does not access your:

  • Contacts or call logs
  • Location
  • Camera or microphone
  • Files or media
  • Network or internet connectivity

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Because no data is collected from any user, the App is safe for use by all ages.

Data Retention

Your data is retained on your device for as long as the App is installed. You can delete individual entries within the App, or remove all data by clearing the App's storage or uninstalling it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@bytebrewinc.com.
